Rules

  1. One should be there in the allocated time Keeping in mind that the hackathon will last for 13 hours.
  2. One should Have his/her own devices {laptops, mobile phones, tab} for the program.
  3. No preinstalled scripts can be used for the program.
  4. One can seek help from internet.
  5. You can participate if and only if you are from grade 9 to 12 or A2(If A levels).
  6. No external storage devices like pen drive and cds will be entertained.
  7. To participate, there ought to be the group of three (3) pupils.

Project Generation Rules

  1. A team, as stated earlier, of 3, should generate a project on their own and try to tackle it out promoting teamwork.
  2. The projects related to data science and machine learning will be entertained.
  3. Algorithm should be made and kept in pdf format.
  4. You should create a presentation on how you tackled with the issues on the project.
  5. Each teammate ought to have a role in issue solving and project creating.

Hackathon Time Period: 13 Hours (starting at 7 am to 8 pm)
